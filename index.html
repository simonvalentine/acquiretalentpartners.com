About Acquire Talent Partners

Building What's Next Since 1987

Founded in Singapore and now headquartered in London, Acquire Talent Partners has grown from a regional systems integrator into one of the world's leading technology and infrastructure companies. We partner with governments, enterprises, and communities to solve complex challenges through innovation.

Our 102,000-strong team combines deep local expertise with global capability, delivering solutions that create lasting value for our clients and the communities where we operate.
